My top 3 movies/shows

Jurassic World: Camp Cretaceous

Six campers are invited to a once in a lifetime opportunity to visit Camp Cretaceous before it's opened to the public. However, things take a turn for the worse when the Indominous Rex escapes captivity.

Jurassic World: Chaos Theory

Set six years after Camp Cretaceous, the campers have now safely returned home - except, now there are dinosours in the real world too and humans must learn to coexist with them but there's a problem - they're being hunted and someone intentionally killed one of the campers and used a dinosour attack to cover it up.

Free Rein

Zoe is on summer vacation when she develops a close bond with a wild horse named Raven. Zoe decides to not return to home after the break and stays with Raven. She also makes close friends but there's tragedy around the corner...
